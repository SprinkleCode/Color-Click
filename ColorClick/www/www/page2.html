Sexual orientation is a person who is sexually, romantically, and emotionally attracted to. Sexual orientations include gay, lesbian, bisexual, asexual, pansexual, and questioning. The term bisexual means that a person is attracted to both females, and males. Asexuality is when a person is not sexually attracted to someone, and does not want a sexual relationship with someone but could have a romantic and emotional relationships with people. Pansexuality is someone who's sexual, and romantic relationships is not limited to biological sex, gender, or gender identity. Those are just a few examples of the different kinds of sexual orientations, there are many more out there. Now, gender indentity is not whp ypu are attracted to, but about who YOU ARE. Transgender is when a person's assigned sex is different from the gender they identify as, and genderqueer is someone who can identify as anything other than cisgender.
