Advice On Being Your Own Black Girl

Carrying Yourself

Carrying yourself means that the way you represent yourself should be based on your own character. The way you carry yourself shows everyone who you are as a person. It shows them that that you are not ashamed to be black. African Americans should carry themselves with high respect towards themselves and one another. This is what carrying yourself with high standards and appreciation for black girls looks like.

A black girl has respect for herself and exemplifies high moral character. She is positive and spreads her positivity to everyone around her. Every time she is down, she is able to pick herself back up, weather its from help from others, or herself. Because she is all this, she never treats others in a way she wouldnt want to be treated.
